Privacy Policy

The Short Version

We only collect the minimum amount of personal information that is necessary to fulfill the purpose of your interaction with us. We do not sell your information to third parties. We do not share your information with third parties. We only use this information as this Privacy Policy describes.

This short version does not tell you everything, so please read on for more details.

What Information We Collect and Why

Information from Web Browsers

When you browse this website, we collect the same basic information that most websites you visit collect. This includes the use of common internet technologies such as cookies or web server logs.

This information may include the following: (1) the visitor's browser type, (2) language preference, (3) referring site, (4) additional websites requested, and (5) the date and time of each visitor request. We may also collect potentially personally-identifying information, such as Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. This information is hereinafter referred to as "Collected Information".

Why Do We Collect This?

We collect this information to better understand how visitors to our website use the information and services this website provides, and to monitor and protect the security of the website.

How We Share the Information We Collect

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade Collected Information with third parties for their commercial purposes.

We do not disclose Collected Information outside of this website except in the situations listed in this section or in the section below on "How we respond to compelled disclosures."

We do not share aggregated, non-personally identifying information with others about how this website's users are interacting and using this website. We do not sell this information to advertisers or marketers, and we have no intention of doing so.

We do not host advertising on this website. Embedded content from third party websites may include ads. This includes embedded content from places such as YouTube. While we try and avoid having advertising on this website, we cannot always control what third parties show.

We may share Collected Information with a limited number of third-party vendors when we use said vendors to improve our services and when those third parties have agreed to privacy restrictions similar to our own Privacy Policy. Our vendors may perform services such as payment processing, customer support ticketing, network data transmission, and similar services.

We may share Collected Information if we are involved in a merger, sale, or acquisition. If any such change of ownership happens, we will ensure that it is under terms that preserve the confidentiality of Collected Information. The organization receiving any Collected Information will have to honor any promises we have made in this Privacy Policy.

How We Respond to Compelled Disclosure

We may disclose personally-identifying information or other information we collect about you to law enforcement in response to a valid subpoena, court order, warrant, or similar government order, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our property rights, or those of third parties or the public at large.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will provide notification to Users of material changes to this Privacy Policy by posting a notice on our home page.
